Our Story

Thank you for visiting Gracias Madres! My name is Mary Pham and I am a co-founder of Gracias Madres. I started Gracias Madres in the summer of 2016 after I had moved from Southern California to the Pacific Northwest. I quickly learned that FaceTime wasn’t enough from keeping me from feeling homesick. That’s when Gracias Madres was born.

Since starting Gracias Madres, my mother and sister have joined me in expanding the store collection. We work hard to make sure all of the Gracias Madres merchandise are not just like any item found at your local home decor store, but that they’re better. Our goal is to bring you products that will keep you smiling.

Passion drives everything we do at Gracias Madres. We want to be the best at what we do — and how we do it.

If you have any comments or questions, we would love to hear from you!
